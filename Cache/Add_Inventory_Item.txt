#Slide: 4
#Header: Properties of Addition
#Content: Addition is commutative (changing the order does not change the result), associative (grouping does not change the result), and has an identity element (0).

#Slide: 5
#Header: Addition Strategies
#Content: Common addition strategies include using number lines, counting on, and using manipulatives to visualize the addition process.
